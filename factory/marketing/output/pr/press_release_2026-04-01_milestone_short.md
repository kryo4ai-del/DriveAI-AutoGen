DriveAI Factory Surpasses 13 Active Marketing Agents Milestone

Innovative milestone achieved as 13 marketing-specific agents drive strategic outreach

San Francisco, April 1, 2026 -- DriveAI Factory today announced the achievement of a significant milestone: the activation of 13 dedicated marketing agents within its AI-powered infrastructure. This development reinforces the Factory’s commitment to seamless, automated solutions from concept to full-fledged app delivery.

In an ecosystem powered by 110 agents across 17 specialized departments, the integration of these 13 active marketing agents marks a crucial step toward enhancing external communications and outreach effectiveness. These agents are programmed with advanced capabilities, leveraging technologies such as Python, AutoGen AgentChat v0.4+, and Claude AI to streamline the marketing operations within the broader framework of the autonomous AI-app production process. The achievement underscores DriveAI Factory's strategic drive to consolidate its presence in key markets and to deliver precision communication outputs across diverse media channels.

“Our milestone reflects our ongoing commitment to integrating AI into every facet of our operations,” said the CEO via CEO-Gate. “Harnessing the unique potential of each agent enables us to efficiently coordinate our external communications and marketing endeavors, ensuring that our narrative remains consistent and impactful across all platforms.” This new configuration not only optimizes real-time responsiveness but also strengthens DriveAI Factory’s capacity to engage with global audiences while maintaining a professional and measured tone.

### About DAI-Core Factory
The DAI-Core Factory is an autonomous AI-app factory featuring 110 agents (103 of which are active) distributed across 17 departments. From ideation to app deployment, the Factory operates without human developers, utilizing state-of-the-art technologies including Python, AutoGen AgentChat v0.4+, and Claude AI to deliver innovative, scalable solutions.
