## Strategic Summary

> **Naming Collision:** Existing "Echo Match" apps (color memory genre) create low-grade brand confusion. Mitigation: dominate *genre* keywords hard — users who arrive via brand confusion and see a superior Match-3 product convert. File this under accidental discovery benefit.

> **Category Ownership Opportunity:** "adaptive puzzle game" and "personalized puzzle game" are near-zero competition. EchoMatch should aim to **define** these terms in both stores before competitors move in — especially as AI gaming becomes a recognized App Store category.

> **US Tone Alignment:** All keyword selections favor informal, intent-driven US search language ("no pay to win", "addictive", "brain"). Avoid formal descriptors ("sophisticated", "intelligent") in metadata — reserve those for PR copy.
